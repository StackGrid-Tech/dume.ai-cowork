# Dume Cowork

**AI Agent That Works While You Don’t**

Dume Cowork is an autonomous AI desktop agent that executes real work on your computer — organizing files, building spreadsheets, generating reports, and running multi-step workflows — so you don’t have to.

> No coding. No setup. No prompt engineering.

---

## 🚀 What is Dume Cowork?

Most AI tools help you *think*.  
Dume Cowork helps you *do*.

Dume Cowork is a **local-first AI agent** that:
- Understands tasks in plain English
- Plans the steps required
- Executes them across your files, apps, and browser
- Asks for approval before major actions

Think of it as a **digital coworker** that actually gets work done.

---

## ✨ Key Capabilities

### 🗂 Organize Files
- Clean messy folders like Downloads
- Sort files by type, date, or project
- Rename and structure automatically

### 📊 Build Spreadsheets
- Extract data from receipts, invoices, or documents
- Generate structured CSV or Excel reports

### 📝 Prepare Reports
- Pull data from tools like GitHub, JIRA, Slack
- Generate formatted reports with summaries and insights

### 📚 Analyze Documents
- Read notes, PDFs, or folders
- Extract action items, decisions, and key insights

### 🤖 Autonomous Workflows
- Break tasks into steps
- Execute end-to-end workflows
- Run while you focus elsewhere

### 📱 Remote Control (WhatsApp)
- Send tasks from your phone
- Dume executes them on your desktop

---

## ⚙️ How It Works

1. **Tell Dume what you need**
   - Type in plain English
   - Drop files or folders
   - Or send via WhatsApp

2. **Dume plans and executes**
   - Breaks tasks into steps
   - Works across files, apps, and browser

3. **You stay in control**
   - Reviews plan before major actions
   - Approve, modify, or cancel anytime

---

## 🔒 Privacy & Security

- **Runs locally on your machine**
- Only accesses folders you allow
- No background scanning
- No data leaves your device without your knowledge
- Always asks before making changes

## 🛠 Installation (macOS)

1. Download the `.dmg` or `.zip` from Releases
2. Open and drag **Dume Cowork** into Applications
3. Launch the app
4. Allow permissions in:
   - `System Settings → Privacy & Security`
5. Sign in or create your account
